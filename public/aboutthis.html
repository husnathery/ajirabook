Dirajumla Publishers

Swahili novels & plays — publishing for entertainment, learning, and life skills

Registered publisher • Operating since 2020 • Head office: Sumbawanga • Branch: Dar es Salaam

Dirajumla Publishers began operations in 2020 with a clear aim to widen access to quality Swahili books. The company focuses on novels and stage plays written in Swahili and provides support to both emerging and established writers who want to create works that entertain, teach, and build practical abilities readers can use in daily life. Publications are selected and produced so they speak to common experiences, spark the imagination, and encourage useful habits.

The company provides end-to-end publishing support. Writers receive manuscript assessment, editing, proofreading, and cover design. Dirajumla also manages printing, distribution, and display planning so books reach retail outlets, schools, libraries, and online shops. Each step is handled with attention to readability, accurate language use, and a tidy presentation that suits learning settings as well as general reading.

Work is organised so editors, designers, and authors collaborate closely during production. This keeps standards consistent while preserving each writer’s voice. Decisions about layout and type focus on the reader’s experience: clear typography, sensible margins, and covers that communicate the story without clutter.

Key services

  • Manuscript review and line editing
  • Proofreading and copy editing
  • Cover and interior design
  • Printing and distribution
  • Marketing and school outreach
  • E-book preparation

Beyond publishing, Dirajumla runs writing workshops, school visits, and mentorship for young authors. These activities help learners and new writers gain confidence and practical skills in storytelling and drama. Selected titles are prepared for digital distribution to reach more readers who rely on mobile devices and tablets.

Our vision

To be a trusted source for Swahili books by growing reading habits, raising writer confidence, and supporting stories that entertain and teach useful skills. Dirajumla aims to be a stable home for creators who wish to share knowledge, encourage imagination, and bring meaningful Swahili titles to communities across Tanzania and beyond.
