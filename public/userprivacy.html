Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 29, 2024

This policy explains what personal data Dirajumla Publishers collects, why it is collected, and how it is used. We value your privacy and follow the requirements of Tanzanian data protection rules where applicable.

1. Information we collect

  • Phone number: collected when a user registers an account.
  • Basic account details such as name when provided by the user.
  • Information you give when contacting us for support or publishing enquiries.

2. Why we collect this information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To send a one-time verification code (OTP) confirming the phone number.
  • To communicate about accounts, book orders, and support.
  • To share updates or promotional messages related to our publishing services if the user agrees to receive them.

3. How we protect your data

Data is kept securely and only accessed by authorised staff for tasks related to the services you request. We do not sell personal data to any third party.

4. Sharing of information

We may share data with service companies that help deliver text message verification, book delivery, or payment services. These partners are required to keep information secure and use it only for the service provided.

5. Data retention

Personal information is stored for as long as the user maintains an account or as required by law for business or tax records. Users may ask for their account to be removed.

6. User rights

  • Request access to the data we hold about them.
  • Request correction of incorrect info.
  • Request removal of their data where legal rules allow.
  • Stop receiving marketing messages at any time by contacting us.

7. Children

Accounts and purchases should be handled by adults or with the involvement of a guardian. If a child’s information is mistakenly collected, contact us for removal.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact Dirajumla Publishers through our Sumbawanga office or Dar es Salaam branch.
